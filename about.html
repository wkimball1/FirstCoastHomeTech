About First Coast Home Tech

Friendly, in-home tech help for real life — from a neighbor who gets it.

Why We Do This

First Coast Home Tech started with a simple idea: tech support shouldn't feel intimidating or corporate. It should feel like having a knowledgeable friend come over — someone who explains things clearly, never talks down to you, and actually cares whether your Wi-Fi works and your mom can video-call the grandkids.

I'm a First Coast resident. I've seen too many people struggle with routers, smart TVs, and "simple" setups that aren't simple when you're not tech-savvy. I started this business because I believe everyone deserves patient, jargon-free help in their own home. No call centers, no scripts — just real support from someone who lives in your community.

What You Can Expect

When we show up, we're not in a rush. We take the time to understand what's going wrong (or what you're trying to achieve) and we explain what we're doing so you feel confident. We're clear about pricing before we start, and we treat your home and your devices with respect. Whether you're on Amelia Island, in Fernandina Beach, Yulee, or anywhere in Nassau County and North Jacksonville, we're here to be your local tech partner.

Patient & Clear

We explain in plain English and never make you feel behind the curve. Questions are welcome.

Community-Focused

We serve our neighbors on the First Coast. Local, reliable, and invested in our area.

Honest Pricing

You'll know the cost upfront. No hidden fees or pressure to buy extras you don't need.
